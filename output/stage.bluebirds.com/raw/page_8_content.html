How Redpanda Seamlessly Generates Pipeline with Bluebirds

Redpanda is a startup building in the data streaming space. With a team of 4 ADRs and 12 AEs, they struggled to efficiently target the right accounts and personalize their outreach at scale. Roman Kopytko, Director of Growth at Redpanda, shares how Bluebirds transformed their sales process, leading to remarkable improvements in efficiency and output.

Setting the Stage: Redpanda's GTM Motion

Redpanda's pipeline is generated from a combination of outbound and inbound efforts. They have a push on SEO, lead magnets on marketing websites, webinars, regularly attend conferences, host dinners, and a variety of other mechanisms to drive inbound leads.

But about half of their pipeline was generated from outbound efforts, and there was an opportunity to improve this process.

The Challenge: Inefficient and Inaccurate Targeting

Before implementing Bluebirds, Redpanda's sales team faced several obstacles:

  1. Incomplete and inaccurate account data
  2. Manual, time-consuming research processes
  3. Inconsistent criteria for account targeting
  4. Inability to identify their ideal customer profile (ICP) effectively
"Our data was all over the place," Roman explains. "We didn't have a distinct account list with territories, and we lacked complete data on account size and employee numbers. We were depending on data from Clearbit and ZoomInfo, but it was insufficient for identifying our ICP.” Beyond that, there were very specific requirements like identifying accounts “implementing large Kafka workflows”.

This led the ADR team to manually research accounts and people, looking for a variety of signals to identify if the prospect would be a good fit for Redpanda. This included:

  • Company tech stack
  • Responsibilities by looking at Linkedin Profiles
  • Engagements on relevant social posts
  • Job postings with relevant keywords
  • Recent venture funding

When the data wasn't available, reps resorted to “vibes-based” prioritization using limited account information – “this was inconsistent and not scalable” says Roman. Beyond that, there was always a fear that existing data sources couldn't actually identify all potential customers with the filters available.

The Goal: Scalably find the “right person at the right place at the right time”

Roman was looking for potential solutions that would allow their team to scalably track champion movement, job changes, recent hires, responsibilities, tech stack, and other signals that his team was looking up manually.

There are a handful of point-solutions that allow you to retrieve one of these data points, but there would have been significant work required to bring them all into Salesforce in a clean way. Roman noted “We would have had to manually power the automation, it's a very tedious process”. Many of the point-solution tools also didn't allow you to build an account list, so he was looking at building a pretty complex architecture to give his team what they needed to succeed.

There was also the question of data accuracy. In a few data tests, the sales team noticed errors with some of the industry classifications. “It's hard to operate a GTM motion if you are providing data that the sales team does not trust.”

The Solution: Bluebirds' Comprehensive Data Intelligence

Within minutes of getting started on Bluebirds, Redpanda built out their Target Account list and found “3,000 accounts that weren't in our database, but should have been”. After that, Roman starting enriching the Account list with relevant signals for prioritization.

From there, the waterfall enriched the buyer personas and loaded them directly into Salesforce for the ADR team to start prospecting. The signals provided by Bluebirds allowed reps to scale personalization, which made a big impact. “On the receiving end, a prospect gets the feeling that this person really understands my needs and business.” Roman notes Bluebirds is able to provide this “in a way that no other database can provide”.

Key Results

1. Improved Account Targeting: Redpanda identified 3,000 new accounts that fit their ICP, which were previously unknown to them.

2. Enhanced Signal Personalization: Roman explains "Being able to get information on the person level in terms of what they do at their organization in their own words is super key. It gives us contextual information that we can use in outbound."

3. Increased Rep Output: ADRs no longer need to manually research signals, allowing them to increase their overall activities.

4. Increased Marketing Efficiency: The tighter high-signal account list improved ad spend efficiency across marketing channels, leading to higher engagements and lower click through rates.

5. Reduced Data Spend: With the ability to consolidate the point-solution providers, Redpanda was able to reduce their total data budget.

Conclusion

By implementing Bluebirds, Redpanda has transformed its go-to-market strategy, achieving greater efficiency and effectiveness in their sales and marketing efforts. The platform's comprehensive data intelligence has enabled them to target the right accounts, personalize their outreach, and align their sales and marketing teams more effectively.

Roman summarizes the impact: "It's incredibly powerful. There is so much you can do with this that would not be possible before. Being able to be very impactful and strategic in your spend and targeting and where you are allocating your resources is incredibly powerful".

For companies looking to revolutionize their sales and marketing processes, Bluebirds offers a powerful solution that provides deep insights and enables data-driven decision-making at scale.
