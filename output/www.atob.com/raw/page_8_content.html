Why AtoB for over the road fleets?

Designed to go the distance

The more you fuel, the more you save

Long-distance and OTR fleets save significantly more money with the AtoB OTR Fuel Card. Enjoy deep discounts on fuel purchases of 45¢ off per gallon on average and up to $2.00 off per gallon* at stations like TA, TA Petro, TA Express, 7-Eleven, Speedway, and many more.*

Find fuel savings along your pre-planned route

Don’t drive extra miles for fuel discounts. The AtoB App factors in real-time visibility on the best offers for savings on fuel expenses available along a driver's route.

Monitor suspicious fuel purchases

Get real-time alerts anytime a truck driver tries to make an unauthorized purchase to keep costs down. Through our Telematics integration, Secure Card Unlock, and custom purchase controls, AtoB will validate every transaction while you focus on managing your fleet.

Hundreds of contractors, one payroll portal

Nationwide fleets are a lot to manage, but at least the payroll can be simple. AtoB processes driver payments 24/7, automates your tax reporting, and offers your drivers on-demand access to all of their payroll information.

Key features

A system built for over the road fleets

Discounts available nationwide

Enjoy discounts and savings on diesel fuel at any participating station. Stop wasting time and miles looking for “in-network” stations. Fill up at virtually any gas station or truck stop nationwide, with average discounts of 45¢ and up to $2.00 off per gallon with AtoB.*

Custom controls and maximum visibility

Set spending limits, add operational hours, and whitelist merchants. AtoB provides you full visibility into every dollar spent and saved, putting you in the driver’s seat of your finances.

Help prevent fuel fraud and theft

Custom controls paired with security features like our Telematics integration and Secure Card Unlock allow us to validate every purchase, helping prevent card misuse and fuel theft.

Route all your 1099 payroll through one platform

Take care of your drivers with AtoB’s Instant 1099 Payroll. Send emergency funds, pay drivers by mileage, hour, or fixed rate and make tax season easier with automated tax reports.
