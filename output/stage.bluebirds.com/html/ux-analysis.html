UX Issue

**Broad Data Collection Categories:** The policy uses very broad terms for data collection, such as "information from your customer relationship management and other platforms and systems" and "publicly available information on the internet, from our partners, service providers and other third parties, and through our independent research." This lacks specificity regarding the types of personal information collected, making it difficult for users to understand the full scope of data collection.
Page: https://stage.bluebirds.com/privacy-policy

UX Issue

**Contradiction/Ambiguity on Data Sale/Sharing (CCPA):** Section 5 states, "We do not sell any personal information collected from your use of or interactions with the bluebirds.ai service," yet Section 9, under "Personal Information We Sold, Shared or Disclosed," explicitly lists categories of personal information "sold or shared to third parties," including "Bluebirds.ai Customers." This creates a significant contradiction or at least ambiguity regarding the company's data sharing practices under CCPA's broad definition of "sale" and "sharing."
Page: https://stage.bluebirds.com/privacy-policy

UX Issue

**Unclear Role of "Bluebirds.ai Customers" in Data Sharing:** The table in Section 9 indicates that personal information is "sold or shared" with "Bluebirds.ai Customers" for the purpose of "Providing Services." The nature of this sharing with the company's own customers is not clearly explained, raising questions about whether Bluebirds.ai is facilitating data sharing between its users or with its users for their own independent purposes, and the implications for data subjects.
Page: https://stage.bluebirds.com/privacy-policy

UX Issue

**Reliance on "Legitimate Interest" for Promotional Communications:** The policy states a "legitimate interest" for promotional communications, implying consent is not usually needed. While valid in some contexts (e.g., existing customer relationships for similar products), this broad reliance might not comply with stricter opt-in consent requirements for direct marketing in certain jurisdictions (e.g., for new contacts or specific types of electronic marketing).
Page: https://stage.bluebirds.com/privacy-policy

UX Issue

**Broad "Third Parties and Connected Services" Sharing:** Section 5 mentions sharing with "Third parties and connected services, including instant messaging and social media platforms you choose to utilize as part of the service" without specifying the types of data shared or the exact purpose beyond the general "as part of the service."
Page: https://stage.bluebirds.com/privacy-policy
