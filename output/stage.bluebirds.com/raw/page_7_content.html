How InterSystems Transformed Their Technical Buyer Outreach with Bluebirds

InterSystems, a vendor of data platforms, information systems, and other solutions has been a leader in the space since 1978. Well known in healthcare, InterSystems also powers some of the most critical applications in financial services, government, and other markets. Brian Sohns, BDR Manager for North America, shares how Bluebirds has improved their sales process, leading to increased efficiency and more targeted outreach.

Setting the Stage: InterSystems' Go-to-Market Strategy

InterSystems go-to-market strategy is customer-centric, focusing on addressing challenges within their customer base. This means that their Account Executives maintain existing accounts while closing new customers. "Our AEs have to act as both account managers and new logo salespeople, handling between 25 and 30 active accounts, plus another 25 or 30 new logo accounts," Brian explains. Each BDR supports about 5-6 Account Executives.

InterSystems buyer is highly technical and sales cycles can take years, so strong targeting of the correct persona is critical for BDR success.

Before implementing Bluebirds, InterSystems sales team faced several key obstacles:

  1. Complex buyer personas across target customers (e.g. healthcare)
  2. Large account lists with limited information
  3. Increasing goals to build larger pipeline at a faster rate

The Goal: Proactive Selling and Larger Pipeline Generation

InterSystems recognized the need to become a more proactive selling organization to build a larger pipeline at a higher rate. They were already using a combination of traditional sales platforms to find ICPs, identify intent, and enrich their data, but there was an opportunity to create a more “streamlined repeatable process” that allows their BDR team to operate more effectively.

The company was looking for a solution that could:

  1. Help ramp up BDRs more efficiently
  2. Get sellers in front of their target personas more effectively
  3. Shorten the sales cycle
  4. Support full-cycle sellers in seeing opportunities from inception to completion

The Solution: Bluebirds

Bluebirds caught the attention of InterSystems leadership with its messaging and trigger-based approach. The platform's ability to help identify and reach the right personas within complex organizations was particularly appealing.

Bluebirds was used to build an Account list, add signals, enrich with their buyer circle, and load into the CRM for the BDR team to use. The enrichment of people has been the most critical feature, as reps were historically spending significant manual time researching the best person.

This was made possible by Bluebird's AI persona model, which maps out titles + responsibilities and finds lookalike best-fit prospects within each account. Bluebirds ultimately helped drive several key results:

1. Enhanced Persona Targeting: Bluebirds has been instrumental in helping InterSystems identify and reach technical buyers. "The personas are really helping fill that inventory of people to call without spending too much time on LinkedIn or other tools to source those people," Brian notes. InterSystems is evolving its approach from solely targeting technical personas to including business-side decision-makers. Bluebirds helps identify individuals who recognize gaps in their business due to data accessibility or understanding issues.

2. Improved Signal Identification: The platform allows InterSystems to go beyond standard LinkedIn filters, helping them identify potential buyers based on their attitudes and openness to change (i.e. by engaging with relevant Linkedin content) rather than just job titles.

3. Increased BDR Efficiency: Not only do BDRs save time on manually finding signals, but the information from the Bluebirds signals allows them to be more confident on the phones, increasing outbound conversion rates.

4. Better Messaging Iteration: Bluebirds is expected to help InterSystems quickly aggregate data and "read between the lines," allowing for faster iteration on messaging and outreach strategies.

Conclusion

By implementing Bluebirds, InterSystems is transforming its approach to finding and engaging technical buyers. The platform has enabled them to target the right personas more effectively, evolve their targeting strategy, and set the stage for more efficient and repeatable outreach processes.

Beyond that, the increased information from signals allows BDRs to be more confident on the phones. Brian thinks Bluebirds will allow him to better create “positive churn” within the organization, where BDRs are moving into other parts of the company to continue their careers.

Brian summarizes the impact: "If you know what you want to accomplish and you're not sure how you want to get the scale, that's where you need to consider Bluebirds. It gives you that flexibility and scalability that is needed to act as a BDR".

For companies looking to revolutionize their approach to finding and engaging technical buyers, Bluebirds offers a powerful solution that provides deep insights and enables data-driven outbound at scale.
