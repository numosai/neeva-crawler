      LeadChain for Bigin

      Experience the power of lead nurturing

      We've developed an innovative solution to revolutionize the way you synchronize and manage leads generated from your social media ads. Explore our key features below to see how LeadChain can empower your lead generation efforts:

       
       
         
          
             LeadChain
      Lead Sync

      Bid farewell to manual lead entry and data silos

      With LeadChain, you can easily capture leads directly from your social media ads, eliminating friction and increasing conversion rates. Our lead sync feature automatically transfers leads captured from your social media ads directly to your CRM in real time, ensuring that no lead is left behind.

      Audience Targeting

      Reach the right people with the right message

      Our advanced targeting capabilities enable you to reach the right audience with your social media ads, thereby driving higher engagement and better results for your campaigns. Optimize your advertisement efforts by targeting the right audience.

      Custom Audiences

      Tailor and target your ad campaigns perfectly

      Create custom audiences using LeadChain's intuitive interface, allowing you to retarget existing leads or reach lookalike audiences with similar characteristics.

      Conversion API

      Get precise tracking of lead conversions

      Maximize the accuracy and reliability of your lead tracking with LeadChain's conversion API integration. By directly connecting your CRM to your social media platforms, our software ensures that every lead conversion is accurately attributed and that the lead lifecycle is taken care of, providing you with invaluable insights into your campaign performance.

      Conversion Leads

      Track the journey of your leads from initial contact to conversion

      Gain visibility into lead interactions, engagements, and touchpoints across channels, allowing you to tailor your needs and and refine the quality of the leads further received, thereby increasing your ROI.

      Find your ideal solution in lead generation

      Meta (Facebook and Instagram)

      LeadChain is tightly integrated with Meta, offering seamless synchronization with Facebook Lead Ads, Custom Audiences, Conversion APIs, and Conversion Leads. Harness the power of Facebook and Instagram's vast user base and advanced advertising features to drive meaningful results for your business.

       
       

      LinkedIn

      Leveraging our integration with LinkedIn, LeadChain enables synchronization with LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms, Matched Audience, and Offline Conversion. Reach professionals and decision-makers on the world's largest professional networking platform and drive quality leads for your business.

      TikTok

      LeadChain creates an automated bridge between your CRM platform and TikTok Lead Generation for seamless tracking, syncing, and nurturing of leads.
